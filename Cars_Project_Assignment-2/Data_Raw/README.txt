Name: The name or model of the car.
Location: The city where the car is located.
Year: The manufacturing year of the car.
Kilometers_Driven: The number of kilometers the car has been driven.
Fuel_Type: The type of fuel the car uses (e.g., Diesel, Petrol).
Transmission: The type of transmission (e.g., Manual, Automatic).
Owner_Type: The type of ownership (e.g., First, Second).
Mileage: The fuel efficiency of the car, typically in kmpl (kilometers per liter) or km/kg (kilometers per kilogram).
Engine: The engine displacement of the car in CC (cubic centimeters).
Power: The power of the engine in bhp (brake horsepower).
Seats: The number of seats in the car.
New_Price: The price of the car when new.
Price: The current price of the car.
